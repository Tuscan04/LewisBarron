First, a little about me

I was born and grew up in Portsmouth, UK. I now live in Wellington, New Zealand where I have resided for just over ten years.

I am a Front-end Web Developer, currently working for Fairfax Media, where I spend most of my time maintaining and developing new features for Stuff.co.nz. I enjoy working on the web, learning new technologies, and meeting new people.

Outside of the world of Web Development, I am a keen traveller, an open-water diver, a gamer, and a sky diving survivor.

Anyway, enough about me, lets carry on scrolling shall we?

Work

I have 6+ years experience as a front-end developer, currently living in Wellington, NZ. I specialise in bridging the gap between designer and back-end programmer, and bringing designs to life. Although I have touched on almost all areas of Web Dev at one time or another, my passion lies in UI development.

I have commercial experience working with some of today's latest technologies, such as HTML5, CSS3, and Sass, as well as a confident understanding of mobile first responsive design and progressive enhancement. I can work with raw JavaScript along with the various libraries such as Jquery and Prototype, and I also have experience working with a number of different content management systems, from Wordpress and Drupal, to Adobe CQ and eZ Publish.

I have worked on various different kinds of projects over the years, from small sites like this one and Kelfezond.co.uk, to taming large news websites like Stuff.co.nz and Essentialmums.co.nz, to strict E-Govt websites like tki.org.nz and sciencelearn.org.nz.
